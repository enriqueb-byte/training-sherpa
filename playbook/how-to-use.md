# How to use the OS Playbook

## Principles

- **One primary objective per 30-minute session.** Secondary depth only if time and energy allow.
- **Paths are defaults.** Skip, compress, or defer using **evidence** (see each module card), not habit.
- **Help Center = how.** This playbook = **why, when, and what “enough” looks like.**
- **Success** = value, confidence, calm, not “every module completed.”

## Evidence hierarchy (engagement)

Use a **subset** per SP; do not require every signal.

1. **Strongest:** Real **customer/job data** in Jobber (not empty setup).
2. **Strong:** **Money movement** (paid invoices, including **outside Jobber Payments**).
3. **Supporting:** Accurate **products/services**, **live website**, **Google Business Profile**, other observable business activity.

If Tier 1 is weak, advanced modules are usually a poor use of the next 30 minutes.

## Baseline “safe” proficiency

Adapt to their model:

- They **use** the chain that fits them: request → quote → job → invoice (or straight-to-job, invoicing elsewhere, etc.).
- Where relevant: **jobs with team assigned.**
- **Active client communications** (observable: messages, reminders, Client Hub, responses, not “templates set once”).

## After each session

- Update [`journey-record/TEMPLATE.md`](../journey-record/TEMPLATE.md) for that SP.
- Pull **Help Center bundle** from the module card into the follow-up email.

## Cadence reminder

~90 days max, many ~75 days; weeks 1–4 most intense (KO + 1–3 trainings, ≥ weekly touch); then ~every 2 weeks. Relationship-driven.
