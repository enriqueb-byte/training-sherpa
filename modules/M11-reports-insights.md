# M11: Reports and insights

**Module ID:** `M11`  
**Typical phase:** Optimization  
**Default prerequisites:** Enough data in account for reports to mean something

### Learning outcome

The SP can open **the few reports that drive decisions** for their persona (cash, utilization, sales, AR) and knows **where to export** if needed.

### Focus areas (30 minutes)

- **Persona filter:** Sam = **simple AR + basic sales**; Gary = **utilization / team**; Paula/Eric = **margin, pipeline, multi-dimension**.  
- **Walk 1–2 reports** in session; Help Center for definitions.  
- **Reporting categories / tags** if their business needs segmentation, light touch.

### Common failure modes

- **Reporting addiction** with **dirty data**: fix inputs first.  
- **Paralysis** from too many dashboards.

### Skip if …

- **No volume yet**: compress to “when you have 30 days of data, open X.”

### Compress if …

- **One metric** that matters this quarter.

### Defer if …

- **M05–M06** data garbage, cleanse first.

### Done looks like (observable)

- SP names **one number** they will check weekly; knows **where** it lives.
