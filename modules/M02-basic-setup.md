# M02: Basic setup (business details, taxes)

**Module ID:** `M02`  
**Typical phase:** Foundation  
**Default prerequisites:** M01 complete or compressed

### Learning outcome

Core **company identity and money settings** are accurate so quotes, jobs, and invoices reflect the real business and tax expectations.

### Focus areas (30 minutes)

- Business details: name, address, branding touchpoints as needed.  
- **Taxes / regions:** how they charge; align settings with how they actually bill.  
- Any **required** regional or compliance fields your org mandates.  
- Preview how these flow into **quotes and invoices** (conceptually, not a full invoicing lesson).

### Common failure modes

- “We’ll fix taxes later” → wrong invoices under pressure.  
- Mismatch between **where they work** and **tax defaults**.

### Skip if …

- **Fully configured** before onboarding and verified, still **spot-check** tax/region in 5 minutes.

### Compress if …

- Simple sole operator, one region, hit only high-risk fields.

### Defer if …

- They genuinely cannot get tax answers today, document **deadline** on journey record.

### Done looks like (observable)

- Settings match **how they quote and invoice** in practice.  
- No known **wrong region/tax** flag.
