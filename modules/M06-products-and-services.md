# M06: Products and services

**Module ID:** `M06`  
**Typical phase:** Workflow → Expansion  
**Default prerequisites:** M05 in motion (real jobs or imminent)

### Learning outcome

**Line items and services** reflect what they sell so quoting and invoicing are fast, accurate, and professional.

### Focus areas (30 minutes)

- Structure: **services vs. packages** appropriate to their bucket (recurring vs. project).  
- **Pricing** models they actually use, avoid imaginary complexity.  
- **Images / descriptions** if they improve close rate or reduce disputes.  
- Connect to **quotes and jobs**; changes here should speed **M05**.

### Common failure modes

- **Over-building** the catalog before workflow habit exists.  
- **Stale list**: wrong items on every quote.

### Skip if …

- List is **accurate and in active use**: spot-check only.

### Compress if …

- **Top 10** services only; defer long tail.

### Defer if …

- **M05** still theoretical, get one real loop first.

### Done looks like (observable)

- **Accurate catalog** for majority of work; SP quotes from it in practice.
