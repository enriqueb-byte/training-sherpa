# M03: Jobber Payments

**Module ID:** `M03`  
**Typical phase:** Foundation  
**Default prerequisites:** M02 in good shape (taxes credible)

### Learning outcome

The SP understands **how Jobber Payments fits their cash flow**, can take payment in context of invoices, and knows **where to manage payouts and issues** (Help Center depth).

### Focus areas (30 minutes)

- Why payments in Jobber: speed, record-keeping, client experience (tailor to persona).  
- Walk **enablement** path at high level; use Help Center for click-by-click.  
- **Fees / payout timing** at the level they care about, point to HC for detail.  
- If they **decline:** document **external payment** habit; success still = **paid** tracked somehow.

### Common failure modes

- Turning on payments without **invoice discipline**: pair with **M05**.  
- Fear of fees without comparing to **cost of not getting paid**.

### Skip if …

- Already **actively** collecting via Jobber Payments with no issues, **compress** to “any payout questions?”

### Compress if …

- They use payments but need **one** fix (reader, bank, etc.).

### Defer if …

- Legal/accounting **blocks** enablement, note date to revisit; still teach **invoice → paid** behavior.

### Done looks like (observable)

- Payments **on** OR explicit **decline with reason** + alternative documented on journey record.  
- At least one **paid** flow understood (Jobber or documented external).
