# M10: Marketing suite

**Module ID:** `M10`  
**Typical phase:** Expansion → Optimization  
**Default prerequisites:** Stable workflow; **Gary+** or marketing-driven pain

### Learning outcome

The SP knows **which marketing tools exist in Jobber**, how they connect to **leads and reviews**, and what to turn on **first** for their bucket.

### Focus areas (30 minutes)

- **Map to their growth lever:** local SEO / GBP, referrals, email campaigns, reviews, **use current product names** from Help Center.  
- **One campaign or one surface** in session, not the whole suite.  
- **Tie to evidence:** website live? GBP? align messaging with **M06** offerings.

### Defer if …

- **Risk flags** yellow/red on workflow or adoption.

- **One** marketing habit **live** (e.g., review request after job) or clear **dated** plan on journey record.
