# M07: Calendar and scheduling

**Module ID:** `M07`  
**Typical phase:** Workflow → Expansion  
**Default prerequisites:** Jobs are real or scheduled; M05 understood

### Learning outcome

The SP can **schedule and adjust work** in a way that matches **volume and bucket** (one-offs, recurring, routes, dispatch).

### Focus areas (30 minutes)

- **Calendar views** that match their role (owner vs. dispatcher).  
- **Recurring / routes** for outdoor or high-repeat businesses.  
- **Dispatch / urgency** language for mechanical trades.  
- **Assignments** aligned with **M08**; who appears on the schedule.  
- **Buffers, travel, constraints** at the level they need, not full operations consulting in one session.

### Common failure modes

- **Beautiful calendar, empty jobs**: reinforce **M05** first.  
- **Over-complex recurrence** before basics stick.

### Skip if …

- **Already scheduling daily** with few errors, compress to **one** advanced nugget.

### Compress if …

- Single-truck operation, only what they use weekly.

### Defer if …

- **Team permissions** unclear, clarify **M08** before deep dispatch rules.

### Done looks like (observable)

- Work appears on the calendar **as they actually run**; changes propagate without chaos.
