# M08: Team and permissions

**Module ID:** `M08`  
**Typical phase:** Expansion  
**Default prerequisites:** Intention to have multiple people in Jobber; jobs exist or are imminent

### Learning outcome

**Right people** have **right access**: field vs. office, what they can see/edit, and **jobs assigned** reflect reality.

### Focus areas (30 minutes)

- **Roles** at high level; use Help Center for exact permission names (product changes).  
- **Invite / onboarding** path for new users.  
- **What techs need** in the field vs. **what office needs**; minimize over-sharing.  
- **Timesheets / labor** if product and business use them; link to HC.

### Common failure modes

- **Everyone admin** because it was faster, security and mistakes.  
- **Field staff** with no path to **see today’s work**.  
- Training only **office** while **owner never opens** app, pair with adoption plan.

### Skip if …

- **Solo Sam** with no near-term hires, defer; note revisit when first hire.

### Compress if …

- **2–3 people**: set roles once; skip edge cases.

### Defer if …

- **Hire is 60+ days out**: focus on **M05–M07** first.

### Done looks like (observable)

- **Assignments** on jobs match who does the work; permission surprises **none** (no “why can’t I see…”).
