# M01: Discovery (kickoff)

**Module ID:** `M01`  
**Typical phase:** Foundation  
**Default prerequisites:** None (first structured touch)

### Learning outcome

You and the SP share a clear picture of **how the business runs**, **who does what**, **what “job” means to them**, and **what success looks like in the next 30–90 days**; so later modules map to reality.

### Focus areas (30 minutes)

- **First ~15 minutes:** Business questions, services, volume, seasonality, who quotes, who invoices, who is in Jobber daily.  
- **Map their lifecycle** in their words: lead → sold work → scheduled → done → paid (may differ from “request → quote → job → invoice”).  
- **Tag persona + workflow bucket** for routing; note **secondary stress** (e.g., scaling owner still doing everything).  
- **Set expectations:** cadence, 30-minute sessions, follow-up emails with Help Center links, relationship + coaching style.  
- **Surface constraints early:** imports planned?, owner login?, payments mindset?

### Common failure modes

- Rushing to screens before understanding **who** adopts Jobber.  
- Assuming their “job” matches the default quote-heavy path.  
- Missing **corporate invoicing** or **straight-to-job** reality.

### Skip if …

- **Never skip** discovery content; you can **compress** if a prior call covered the same ground, still confirm bucket + lifecycle in 5 minutes.

### Compress if …

- Returning SP or restart: confirm **what changed** and **current blockers** only.

### Defer if …

- N/A, this is the container for defer decisions later.

### Done looks like (observable)

- Journey record has **persona**, **bucket**, **primary contact**, **noted exceptions** (invoicing elsewhere, etc.).  
- You can state their **workflow in one sentence**.
